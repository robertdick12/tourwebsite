about us

Welcome To Ancestral Tours,
your hub for planning, organizing and creating an authentic experience within the country of Ghana. At Ancestral Tours, we focus on the history of the African heritage, lifestyle, and culture, while also providing a pathway forward through the evolution of Ghana, and other neighboring countries within the African continent. Each tour is uniquely designed and customized according to the desires and or needs of each customer. This allows each person to embody their own individual experience of the country and the amazing insight it offers. Our goal at Ancestral Tours is to help visitors become family by providing an authentic experience here in Ghana - one that encompasses our history but embraces our future. Ancestral tours highlight our ‘roots’ so that we can better understand where we have come from in order to know where we are going. A portion of all proceeds from Ancestral Tours is used to provide support to the children of (name of organization) with school supplies each academic school year. Ancestral Tours is founded and based in Cape Coast, Ghana, with an expanded location in Boston, Massachusetts, USA to provide flexibility for our customers who want to learn more and are interested in planning a trip to Ghana.
