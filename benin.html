Benin
Benin, formally known as a Dahomey, is a country in West Africa. It borders with Togo on West, East with Nigeria, North with Burkina Faso and Niger, on the south with Atlantic ocean. The capital is Porto-Nove but the seat of the government is Cotonou with a population of about 11 million. Known for its deep voodoo practices and beliefs that a lot of Haitians slaves were taken from Benin thus sharing a same religious background
